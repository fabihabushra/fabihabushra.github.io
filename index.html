Hi, I’m Fabiha Bushra

AI Robotics Enthusiast

My journey started with pre-programmed robots. I started imagining what if these robots could be more. I did not have direct exposure to AI robotics since it is an unexplored area of research in my part of the world. But the thought of an assistant robot led me to backtrack to the research being done in other parts of the world. I began with deep learning and did a computer vision project! Click here to see what I did.

But I realized that the project had certain limitations which are concerned with the data-driven approach of Supervised Learning. After much digging, I came to understand that the current breakthroughs in AI are a function of time and computational resources. That's why the current robots are only capable of doing repetitive tasks in controlled environments. But they do not have the commonsense to operate in a human-unstructured environment and collaborate with us.

This perspective leads me to my current research interests which are:

  • Robot Perception and Control
  • Deep Reinforcement Learning

    • Currently, I am looking forward to areas such as inverse reinforcement learning, unsupervised reinforcement learning, meta-learning, and hierarchical learning.
